Our Mission

To provide quality education that develops each child's academic, social, emotional, and physical potential in a safe, supportive, and challenging environment.

Our Vision

To be a leading primary school that empowers students to become confident, creative, and responsible global citizens ready for lifelong learning.

Our Values

Excellence, Integrity, Respect, Collaboration, Innovation, and Inclusivity guide everything we do at Kesawai Primary School.

Our Story

Established in 2004, Kesawai Primary School began as a small community initiative with just 50 students and 5 dedicated teachers. Our founders envisioned a school that would provide world-class education while maintaining strong community values.

Over the years, we have grown to serve over 500 students from grades 1 through 8, with a team of 35+ qualified educators. Our commitment to academic excellence, character development, and holistic education has made us a trusted choice for families in the community.

Today, we continue to evolve and adapt our educational approach to meet the changing needs of our students while staying true to our core values of excellence, integrity, and compassion.
